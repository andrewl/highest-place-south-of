Highest place south of...

This is an interactive map of those places that can claim to be 'the highest place in the UK south of (some other place)'
Why? Walking up Pen-y-fan recently I saw it was listed somewhere as 'the highest place south of Cadair Idris' - being a mapping fan I wondered whether it was possible to find out where all the places that were the 'highest place south of' somewhere else.

NOT FOUND

You just hit a route that doesn't exist... the sadness.

Build by Andrew Larcombe using GatsbyJS and using data from OpenStreetMap, images from wikimedia and maps from MapBox
